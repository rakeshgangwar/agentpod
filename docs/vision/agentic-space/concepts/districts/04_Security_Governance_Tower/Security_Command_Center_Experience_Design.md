The Security Command Center transforms cybersecurity and risk management into an immersive fortress environment where digital threats are visualized as approaching armies and security defenses appear as protective barriers. This military-inspired command center enables security teams to monitor, defend, and respond to threats while collaborating with AI agents specialized in threat detection, incident response, and security intelligence.

**Theme**: Medieval Fortress meets Modern SOC meets Cyber War Room
**Primary Function**: Cybersecurity monitoring, threat detection, incident response, risk management
**Capacity**: 20 security professionals + 10 AI agents simultaneously
**Platforms**: VR, AR, Desktop, Mobile with seamless cross-platform collaboration
