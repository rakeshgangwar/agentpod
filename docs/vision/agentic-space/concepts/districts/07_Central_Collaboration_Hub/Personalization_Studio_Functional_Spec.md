# Personalization Studio

## Overview
The Personalization Studio is a comprehensive customization platform that enables deep personalization of the agentic intelligence experience without requiring technical expertise. It empowers users to tailor their interface, workflows, and agent interactions to match their specific roles, preferences, and business contexts.

## Core Purpose
- **Role-Based Customization**: Adapt the platform to specific organizational roles and responsibilities
- **Personal Preference Management**: Enable individual customization based on personal work styles and preferences
- **Context-Aware Adaptation**: Automatically adjust interface and functionality based on current business context
- **Team Standardization**: Enable teams to standardize configurations while maintaining individual flexibility
- **Continuous Learning**: Platform learns from user behavior to suggest optimal configurations

## Key Features

### **1. Role Template Selector**
**Functionality**: Choose and customize base templates optimized for specific organizational roles
- **Comprehensive Role Library**: Pre-built templates for common enterprise roles across all functions
- **Industry Customization**: Role templates customized for specific industries and business contexts
- **Hybrid Role Support**: Templates for users who perform multiple roles or have complex responsibilities
- **Custom Role Creation**: Ability to create entirely new role templates for unique organizational needs

**Role Template Categories**:
- **Executive Templates**: CEO, CFO, COO, and other C-level executive configurations
- **Finance Templates**: Controllers, analysts, accountants, and financial planning roles
- **Operations Templates**: Manufacturing, supply chain, quality, and operational management roles
- **Sales Templates**: Sales representatives, managers, and business development roles
- **Technical Templates**: Developers, system administrators, and IT support roles
- **Specialized Templates**: Compliance, legal, HR, and other specialized function roles

### **2. Industry Configuration Wizard**

- **Regulatory Compliance**: Automatic configuration for industry-specific regulatory requirements

**Industry Configurations**:
- **Manufacturing**: Production planning, quality control, and supply chain optimization
- **Healthcare**: Patient management, regulatory compliance, and clinical workflows
- **Financial Services**: Risk management, regulatory reporting, and customer onboarding
- **Retail**: Inventory management, customer experience, and demand forecasting
- **Professional Services**: Project management, resource allocation, and client management
- **Government**: Compliance tracking, citizen services, and regulatory management

### **3. Personal Preference Engine**
**Functionality**: Individual customization based on personal work styles and preferences
- **Interface Customization**: Personalized layout, color schemes, and information density
- **Interaction Preferences**: Customized agent communication styles and interaction patterns
- **Workflow Preferences**: Personal workflow optimizations and shortcuts
- **Notification Management**: Personalized notification preferences and priority settings

**Preference Categories**:
- **Visual Preferences**: Themes, layouts, fonts, and accessibility settings

- **Information Display**: Dashboard layouts, widget preferences, and data visualization styles
