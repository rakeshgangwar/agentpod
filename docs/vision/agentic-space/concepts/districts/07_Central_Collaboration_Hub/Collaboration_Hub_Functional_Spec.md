# Collaboration Hub

## Overview
The Collaboration Hub is a sophisticated multi-user workspace that enables teams to leverage collective intelligence through shared AI assistants, collaborative decision-making tools, and knowledge sharing platforms. It transforms traditional collaboration by integrating agentic intelligence into every aspect of team interaction.

## Core Purpose
- **Collective Intelligence**: Harness team knowledge and AI capabilities for superior outcomes
- **Shared AI Workspaces**: Teams collaborate with shared AI assistants that learn from group interactions
- **Cross-Functional Coordination**: Break down silos through integrated collaboration tools
- **Knowledge Amplification**: Capture, share, and build upon organizational intelligence
- **Decision Synchronization**: Align team decisions through transparent, AI-assisted processes
