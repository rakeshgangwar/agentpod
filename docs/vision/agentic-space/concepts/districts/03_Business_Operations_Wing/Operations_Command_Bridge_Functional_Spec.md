# Operations Command Bridge

## Overview
The Operations Command Bridge is a real-time operational monitoring and control interface designed for operations managers, plant managers, and operational teams. It provides live visibility into operational performance, immediate exception handling, and coordinated response capabilities across all operational activities.

## Core Purpose
- **Live Operational Dashboard**: Real-time monitoring of all operational activities and performance
- **Exception Alerting & Resolution**: Immediate identification and resolution of operational exceptions
- **Resource Allocation & Scheduling**: Dynamic resource allocation and operational scheduling
- **Performance Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring and optimization of operational performance
- **Quality Control & Assurance**: Real-time quality monitoring and control processes
