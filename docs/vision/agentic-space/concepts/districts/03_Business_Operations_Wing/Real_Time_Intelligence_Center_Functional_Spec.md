# Real-Time Intelligence Center

## Overview
The Real-Time Intelligence Center is a live monitoring and immediate response platform that provides instant visibility into critical business events, real-time decision support, and coordinated emergency response capabilities. It serves as the nerve center for time-sensitive operations and crisis management.

## Core Purpose
- **Real-Time Event Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring of business events and critical processes
- **Immediate Alert Processing**: Instant processing and routing of critical alerts and notifications
- **Live Decision Support**: Real-time decision support for urgent business situations
- **Emergency Response Coordination**: Coordinated response to emergency and crisis situations
- **Critical Process Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring of mission-critical business processes
