# Data Management Studio

## Overview
The Data Management Studio is a comprehensive data governance and management platform that provides enterprise-wide data cataloging, quality management, lineage tracking, and privacy controls. It serves as the central hub for all data-related governance activities across the agentic intelligence ecosystem.

## Core Purpose
- **Data Source Management**: Comprehensive cataloging and management of all enterprise data sources
- **Data Quality Management**: Automated data quality monitoring, validation, and improvement
- **Data Lineage Tracking**: Complete visibility into data flow and transformation across systems
- **Master Data Management**: Unified management of master data entities across the enterprise
- **Data Privacy & Protection**: Comprehensive data privacy controls and compliance management
