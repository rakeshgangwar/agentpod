# Data Management Studio - "The Data Garden"
## Multi-Platform Room Experience Design

## üå± Room Overview

The Data Management Studio transforms data governance and management into an immersive cultivation experience where organizational data grows and flourishes like a living ecosystem. This botanical-themed environment enables data teams to nurture, organize, and optimize data assets while collaborating with AI agents specialized in data quality, governance, and lifecycle management.

**Theme**: Botanical Garden meets Digital Library meets Data Laboratory
**Primary Function**: Data governance, quality management, lifecycle optimization, data architecture
**Capacity**: 16 data professionals + 7 AI agents simultaneously
**Platforms**: VR, AR, Desktop, Mobile with seamless cross-platform collaboration
